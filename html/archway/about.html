Message From Founder



Afeef Shaikh, Founder

Education- BSc Interior Design (Cadence Design Academy, Nagpur)

He started his career in 2013 in the interior industry. Specializing in civil work, he has excellent project management and team-handling skills. His design expertise makes him a perfectionist in the interior sector. Early in his career, working with architects helped him understand building structures and bungalow designs. In Nagpur, he has successfully designed numerous bungalows, consistently earning client appreciation. His focus is on delivering projects within a low budget, making dream homes accessible to everyone.
